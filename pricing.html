Frequently asked questions

What happens during the 14-day free trial?

You get full access to all Plus features for 14 days. No payment required upfront. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, you'll be charged for your first billing period.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have Plus access until the end of your current billing period, then automatically switch to the Free plan.

Do you provide receipts and invoices?

Yes, you'll receive an email receipt for every payment. You can also download invoices from your account dashboard for expense reports or tax purposes.

Is there a student discount?

We're working on a student discount program. In the meantime, the Free plan includes all essential budgeting features, and the 14-day Plus trial gives you time to explore advanced features.

How is GST handled?

GST is automatically calculated and included where applicable for Australian customers. International pricing may vary based on local tax requirements.

What happens if I downgrade from Plus to Free?

Your data remains safe. You'll keep access to your transaction history, but some features like multiple budgets and collaborators will be disabled. You can upgrade again anytime to restore full access.
