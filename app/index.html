Quick Help

Getting Started:

  • Set your income amount and frequency at the top
  • Add expense and savings buckets
  • Add items to each bucket with their amounts
  • All amounts are in your chosen frequency (weekly/fortnightly/monthly/yearly)

Features:

  • Click bucket headers to collapse/expand
  • Uncheck "Include" to exclude items from totals
  • Bank account field helps track where money comes from
  • Export/Import for backup and sharing
  • Press Enter in last item row to add new item
  • Press Ctrl/Cmd+Enter to add new bucket

Calculations:

  • Totals update automatically as you type
  • Savings rate shows percentage of income going to savings
  • Leftover shows what remains after planned savings
