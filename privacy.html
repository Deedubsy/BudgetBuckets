Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Budget Buckets is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our budget management application.

Information We Collect

We collect and store the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication data through Firebase Authentication
  • Budget Data: Your budget buckets, income settings, expenses, savings goals, and financial data you enter into the app
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage statistics to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Budget Buckets service
  • Sync your budget data across your devices
  • Authenticate your account and ensure security
  • Improve our app based on usage patterns
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal financial data with third parties. Your budget information remains private and is only accessible to you through your authenticated account.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your budget data using our export feature
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request corrections to your data

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your associated data within 30 days, except where required by law.

Cookies and Tracking

Budget Buckets uses minimal tracking technologies necessary for authentication and app functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, providing additional notice.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Support page.
